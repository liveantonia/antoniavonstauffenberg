Hello, I'm Antonia

I am an astrophysicist with a passion for unravelling the large-scale structure of the cosmos. My research spans observational cosmology, galaxy formation, and the interplay between dark matter and baryonic physics.

Whether I'm analysing survey data from ground-based telescopes or running hydrodynamic simulations, I am driven by the same question: how did the universe come to look the way it does?

When I'm not chasing photons, you'll find me mentoring students, writing outreach pieces, or hiking somewhere with a good view of the night sky.
